KatManDo Google Add-on Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 30, 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our services. By interacting with our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better user experience and improve our services. The types of information we gather include:

  • Log Data: When you visit our website, our servers automatically collect information such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and the time of your visit.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember your preferences and track your interactions with our website. Cookies are small digital files stored by your web browser.
  • Personal Information: If you register on our site, we may collect personal information such as your email address and username.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to enhance your experience on our website and tailor content to your interests. Specifically, we may use your information to:

  • Display personalized content and advertisements.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about our services, updates, and offers.

Sharing Your Information

We value your privacy and do not sell, rent, or lease your email address or other personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners to provide services on our behalf, but only under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Choices

You have control over how your information is used and shared:

  • Unsubscribing: If you have subscribed to our services, you may unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions in the emails you receive.
  • Cookie Management: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your access to certain features of our website.

Security

We take security seriously and implement robust measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The most current version will always be available on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us.
