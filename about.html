CRMantra: Experts in CRM, CPQ, and CLM

We are a curious and dedicated team of consultants, engineers, solution architects, developers and managers. During our previous lives working as product managers, engineers, and sales reps for Siebel (now part of Oracle) we have seen customers struggle to implement complex software and the associated change management. We founded the company to make a difference. Since our founding we have been the go-to guys to fix implementations gone awry. This applies to implementations of CRM Sales and Service, CPQ (Configure, Price & Quote) and CLM (Contract Lifecycle Management). Our reputation as the top-notch specialists has led many customers to call upon us to get it right the first time.
